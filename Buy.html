How To Purchase

MicroKey Main Page Products Q&A

Step One:

Note down the type of product you want to buy and the different options (eg. Case color, Switch, Keycap)

Step Two:

Send an email to 2215032@student.isf.edu.hk with the subject of "Purchase Request" and be sure to write down your name, tutor group, order information (product you want to buy, case color, etc.)

Step Three:

Check for a email with the subject of "Purchase Confirmed" in which it will give you information on the estimated time for your product to be delivered to your tutor at tutor time.

Step Four:

Prepare the right amount of money and when we deliver the product, give the delivery guy the money.
